Pint of Science Festival

I had a fantastic time organising the Manchester 2023 Pint of Science festival this year. Pint of Science is an annual science communication and public engagement festival held worldwide, providing a place for researchers and industry experts to present their work informally in pubs, caf√©s, and bars.

We held three consecutive nights at the English Lounge in Manchester's Northern Quarter. Our first explored wearable technologies, our second the fuels of the future, and our third shone a light on quantum materials. We had a diverse range of speakers from both industry and academia, including early and late-stage researchers.

Nine months of organising really paid off, with good ticket sales (including one sold-out evening!) and some really eye-opening and engaging discussions. In addition, getting to spend Monday to Wednesday night in the pub for "work" is always a bonus. Pint of Science runs yearly and I would highly recommend trying to attend. There is a huge variety of events catering to all interests - I am looking forward to attending next year!
